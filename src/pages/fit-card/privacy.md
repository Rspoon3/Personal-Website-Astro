# Privacy Policy

**Fit Card** is designed with your privacy in mind. Your workout and health data stays on your device, and the app reads from Apple Health only — it never writes your data and does not send it to external servers.

## Information We Access

To build shareable cards, **Fit Card** may access the following on your device, only with your permission:

- **Apple Health workouts** — workout type, duration, distance, calories, heart rate, steps, and related metrics, plus workout route data for map backgrounds.
- **Photo library** — only the photos you choose to use as a card background.
- **Apple Music** — album and song artwork (via MusicKit) when you select an artwork background. Requires an Apple Music subscription.
- **Location** — used to render a route or current-location map background and to reverse-geocode a workout's location label.
- **Notifications** — if enabled, a local notification to prompt you to share when a workout finishes.

## How We Use Your Data

- **Local Processing:** Everything happens on your device. Cards are generated locally and exported as images you choose to save or share.
- **Read-Only Health Access:** **Fit Card** reads from Apple Health and never writes to it. Your workouts stay on your device.
- **No External Servers:** Your health, photo, music, and location data is not uploaded, stored, or processed on any server controlled by us.

## Information We Do Not Collect

The **Fit Card** app does not collect or store:

- Names or email addresses
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Any data beyond what is needed to build a card on your device

## Your Data Stays Private

- All health data accessed from Apple Health remains on your device.
- Photos, artwork, and location are used only to render the card you are creating.
- Cards you export are saved or shared only where you choose to send them.
- You can revoke Health, Photos, Music, Location, or Notification access at any time in the iOS Settings app.

## Contacting Me

By contacting me in any of the following ways you are explicitly giving details of yourself (such as your account name or email address) and giving permission for me to answer any questions or comments you may have. These messages will possibly be stored, monitored, and used in accordance with the privacy policy of the service(s) used.

## Business Transfers

If **Fit Card**, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of **Fit Card** may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

## Privacy Policy for Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal data while contacting me, please reach out and I will take steps to remove it.

## Privacy Policy Changes

From time to time, this privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be modified accordingly.
